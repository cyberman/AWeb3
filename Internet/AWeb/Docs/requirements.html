System requirements

AWeb needs the following to run:
  • AWeb is built against the NDK 3.2 but should work on Workbench 3.1 (with ClassAct), and 3.5 and 3.9 with ReAction as well, but compatibility has not been tested.

  • At least 3 MB of memory as an absolute minimum. 8 MB or more is highly recommended.

  • AmiTCP/IP or compatible TCP stack to access the World Wide Web. RoadShow and UAE bsdsocket.library are tested and recommended. Other stacks like Miami and TermiteTCP may work but are not tested.
    Note: INet225 and AS225 are deprecated and no longer supported.

  • Unlike earlier distributions of AWeb, image format plugins are now included in the distribution..

  • To hear background sounds on pages, a WAV and an AU datatype are recommended. Instead of sound datatypes, you may want to use an external player like Play16. For MIDI background sounds, a MIDI player (like GMPlay) is recommended.

  • For 24-bit graphics support, P96 (Picasso96) is required.
    Note: CyberGraphics is deprecated and no longer supported.
