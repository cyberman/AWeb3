System requirements

AWeb needs the following to run:
  • AmigaOS 3.2 or better. It may work with OS 3.0 or 3.1, but compatibility is not guaranteed. See also the note below about Reaction, formerly ClassAct.

  • At least 3 MB of memory as an absolute minimum. 4 MB or more is highly recommended.

  • AmiTCP/IP or compatible TCP stack to access the World Wide Web. RoadShow and UAE bsdsocket.library are known to be compatible. Other stacks like Miami and TermiteTCP may work but are not tested.
    Note: I-Net225 and AS-225 are deprecated and no longer supported.

  • Unlike earlier distributions of AWeb, image format plugins are now included..

  • To hear background sounds on pages, a WAV and an AU datatype are recommended. Instead of sound datatypes, you may want to use an external player like Play16. For MIDI background sounds, a MIDI player (like GMPlay) is recommended.

  • For 24-bit graphics support, P96 (Picasso96) is required.
    Note: CyberGraphics is deprecated and no longer supported.

  • AWeb 3.6 is tested against AmigaOS 3.2 and Reaction. It may work with ClassAct or with Reaction from AmigaOS 3.5 or 3.9, but compatibility is not guaranteed.
